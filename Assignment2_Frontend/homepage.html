ABOUT US

Mission

At volunteer central, we foster a community of service, leadership, and community where event planners and volunteers can connect.

What we do

We provide a digital hub where organizers can post events with skill requirements and volunteers can browse and sign up for events they are suited for. By streamlining the process, we help the community mobilize support while volunteers give back.
